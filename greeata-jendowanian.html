Greeata Jendowanian was a female Rodian who worked as a singer and dancer for the Max Rebo Band.

A life in music

In 19 BBY, towards the end of the Clone Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, she attended a performance of a Mon Calamari opera on the planet Coruscant.

Four years ABY, she and the rest of the Max Rebo Band performed for Jabba the Hutt at his palace on the planet Tatooine. The group played the song "Jedi Rocks" for the Hutt, who demanded that they repeat the performance, but then dropped his slave dancer, Oola into his rancor pit during the performance, causing the band to stop playing, leaving them both shocked and annoyed. Greeata was visibly shocked when Oola was eaten by the rancor, and then witnessed Princess Leia Organa arrive in the palace disguised as the bounty hunter, Boushh with the Wookiee, Chewbacca as a prisoner.
